My Education

I am a senior at Arizona State University pursuing a degree in Computer Information Systems. I have interned in Cybersecurity for 6 months and recently got a certificate in Cloud Security Knowledge. All of the certificates and resumes can be found on the documents page of my website.

My educational aspirations include going to graduate school. Here's where I get conflicted though: I have so many things I want to study! When I was young, I wanted to go into microbiology and study infectious diseases. While that is a complete left turn from what I am currently doing, a part of me still dreams about it because I still love to read about epidemiology! I really recommend all of Richard Preston's novels, such as "The Hot Zone" or "The Demon in the Freezer". They're all very fascinating!

Realistically though, if I were to stay closer to my current degree, I would want to stay in cyber security and maybe go on to study cryptography. I am going to get a few years of experience working first, to make sure this is what I want to do, but after that, I fully intent on coming back to school for a Masters. Maybe I will even go on and get a PhD.

For the time being, while I work towards graduation, I am going to simultaneously try and get some certificates. I got my first one on 9/27 on Cloud Security. I might go on and do the security+ next.
